What is this?

This is a tiny reference client for arkA – an open, host-anywhere video protocol. The idea is:

  • Video is stored anywhere (IPFS, S3, NAS, Arweave, etc.).
  • The client just needs a pointer (like an IPFS CID) to play it.
  • No accounts, no algorithmic feed – just metadata + a lightweight client.

MVP scope: one video at a time, identified by a CID or URL. Later we can add full arkA channel metadata, playlists, captions, and more.
